About Melissa Schembri

Melissa Schembri’s track record is impressive. She is a Real Estate Agent, former Property Lawyer, Life Coach, Motivational Speaker and Best Selling Author. Melissa holds a Bachelor of Law and Bachelor of Arts, majoring in Applied Ethics.

A highly driven professional, Melissa opened her own Property Law firm in Mooloolaba at age 25 where she successfully sold her services and gave advice to Property Owners for over $2.4 billion dollars worth of property. During this time, Melissa became an exceptional negotiator and in 2008, was awarded the prestigious title of Australian Institute of Management’s “Young Manager of the Year”.

After selling her law firm in 2009, Melissa transferred her problem solving skills into life coaching and motivational speaking where she excelled at helping her clients achieve their goals on a more personal level.

As a Real Estate Agent, Melissa achieves outstanding results for her clients as a direct result of her previous legal experience, negotiation skills and her ability to build instant rapport with potential Buyers and her Sellers.

One of Melissa’s best traits is that she genuinely cares about people and utilises her highly developed problem solving skills and legal knowledge to achieve a winning outcome.

  • Melissa has settled over $2.4 billion worth of property
  • 20 years of property knowledge and experience
  • Former experienced Property Lawyer to help the entire transaction
